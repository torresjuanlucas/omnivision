Internship

Home - Internship

Omnivision Design, a Web Marketing company, founded by Andreas Deligeorge in Montreal, Quebec (Canada), offers different Web Marketing services such as Website Development, SEO strategy, Google Adwords campaigns, Social Media services, Email Marketing services and Graphic Design services (logos, business cards, flyers).

Since 2011, Omnivision Design has welcomed students from all around the world regarding their respective internships. The company has already worked with students from Canada, USA, Argentina Brazil, China, France, Mexico, Russia and Venezuela.

Whether you are a first year or a graduating student, we will be happy to have you for a online marketing internship in order to introduce you to this field, and to help you develop your knowledge.

We are looking for dynamic, motivated, volunteer students, who want to learn, study and work in the field of marketing. We generally take on interns for a period of 2 to 6 months, although there are some exceptions.

Throughout your internship in Canada, it's also an opportunity to discover a new country with a unique culture. You will also benefit by gaining international experience, through an online marketing internship in Montreal.

Montreal is a cosmopolitan city with a European twist. It is a modern and enjoyable city for any student, especially during spring and summer when many festivals are taking place.

So, if you are interested, contact us by e-mail at the following address: info@omnivisiondesign.com
